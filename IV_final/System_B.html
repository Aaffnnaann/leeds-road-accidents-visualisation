System B
Temporal Exploration of Road Traffic Accidents
Dataset: Leeds Road Traffic Accidents — Kaggle Source ↗
Visualisation Tasks
T1
Explore spatial features [Search → Explore; Target: Feature] — Browse the map; it responds to period and hour selections and feeds back to the hour distribution.
T2
Discover temporal distribution [Analyse → Discover; Target: Distribution] — The hour bar chart shows accident frequency within the selected time-of-day period.
T3
Select a subset and summarise severity [Search → Browse; Query → Summarise] — Period click + hour brush + map brush each define a subset; the severity chart summarises the result.
Generalised Selection
Click a period wedge (donut) to select all hours in that semantic period (Night / Morning Rush / Daytime / Evening Rush / Late Evening). Then drag the hour bar to narrow within the period. Drag the map for spatial refinement. This implements a Hour → Time Period semantic hierarchy traversal.
Comparison Mode
Selection A vs Selection B — Four-Panel Comparison
How to Use
A
Drag on the left chart to brush a range of years for Selection A.
B
Drag on the right chart to brush a range of years for Selection B.
The map overlays both selections (blue = A, orange = B) and the severity chart shows side-by-side counts so you can directly compare the two periods.
